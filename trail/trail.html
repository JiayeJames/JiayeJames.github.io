ABOUT THE COURSE

The Maple Leaf World School Program is an English-language high school program geared toward university preparation for both domestic and international students. It is a program that Maple Leaf Group developed on its own. Through a diverse, dynamic, and engaging learning curriculum that emphasizes academic excellence, students are prepared for success at reputable universities as well as as contributing global leaders and citizens thanks to the innovative curriculum. English is taught to students by certified instructors.

The school is accredited by Cognia (previously AdvancEd) and goes through a strict annual inspection.
